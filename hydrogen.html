About Hydrogen

Hydrogen is the lightest and simplest element and, with a ratio of 80%, is the main ingredient of the visiable universe.
20% consist of the helium, the ratio of heavier element is below 1%.
Most stars, including our Sun, generate energy by fusion of hydrogen to helium.
Hydrogen is quite abundant on Earth too, opposoteto helium, because it is a very reactive element and so is part of many diffrent compunds.
