Carbon


About Carbon

Carbon is the base of all life and the element with the most complex chemistry, which
is called organic chemistry .
Coal, which consists mostly of carbon, is known and used since prehistoric time.
Mineral oil consists largely of hydrocarbons. The combustion of carbon produces carbon dioxide, CO2.
This is a greenhouse gas, which traps heat radiation.
